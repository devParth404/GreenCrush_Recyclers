How We Work

1. Pickup Request & Scheduling

Customers contact us via call or WhatsApp. Our team confirms location, quantity and schedules a convenient pickup time.

2. On-Site Collection

Our trained staff visits your location with proper safety equipment and collects glass bottles carefully.

3. Sorting & Quality Check

Collected glass is separated by type and color. Contaminated material is removed to ensure quality output.

4. Crushing & Processing

Bottles are crushed into chips or fine powder using industrial machines under controlled conditions.

5. Storage & Inventory

Processed materials are stored in secure sacks and warehouses with proper batch labeling.

6. Supply to Industries

Glass products are supplied to construction, cement, brick and manufacturing industries.
