How We Work

Pickup Request

Customers contact us via WhatsApp or call. We collect details such as location, quantity and schedule pickup.

On-Site Collection

Our trained team collects glass using safety equipment ensuring zero damage or spillage.

Sorting & Quality Check

Glass is sorted by color and type. Contaminated material is removed.

Crushing & Processing

Glass is crushed into chips or powder using industrial machines.

Storage & Inventory

Processed material is stored batch-wise with proper labeling.

Supply to Industries

Recycled products are supplied to construction and manufacturing units.
